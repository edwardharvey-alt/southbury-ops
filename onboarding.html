Let's get you ready to accept card payments

Every order placed through your drops is paid by card. Setting up payments means money from orders goes straight to your bank account — usually within 2 business days. Thousands of UK food businesses use Stripe to do this securely.

  • Your bank account sort code and account number
  • A form of ID (driving licence or passport)
  • Your business registration number (if you have one)

Your Stripe account is linked. You're ready to accept card payments through your drops.

Stripe processes card payments for your orders. When a customer places an order through one of your drops, the payment goes through Stripe directly into your bank account. Hearth never holds your money.
Stripe charges 1.5% + 20p per successful UK card transaction. There are no monthly fees, no setup fees, and no minimum commitment. You only pay when you get paid.
Stripe pays out to your bank account on a rolling basis — typically within 2 business days of each transaction. You can see all your payouts in your Stripe dashboard.
Yes. Stripe is PCI Level 1 certified — the highest level of security in the payments industry. Your bank details and your customers' card details are handled entirely by Stripe. Hearth never sees or stores payment information.
Yes. You can update your bank account, business details, and payout schedule at any time through your Stripe dashboard. Hearth doesn't control those settings — they're yours.
Yes. Stripe accepts personal bank accounts for sole traders. During setup, Stripe will ask for your business type — select "Individual / sole trader" and use your personal bank details.
